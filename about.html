Our Story

Opened in 2016, Tokyo Deep Gem began as a tiny record stand tucked away in a Shibuya side street.

We curate an eclectic mix of indie releases, city pop classics, and experimental sounds that capture the soul of Tokyo's underground.

Built by collectors for collectors, our shop focuses on quality pressings and a welcoming vibe for anyone who loves vinyl.
